The Wind Rises

The Wind Rises is a 2013 Japanese animated historical drama film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki, which was animated and published by Ghibli Studio. The story follows and is inspired by the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the main subject of the film. In Jiro's childhood days, his dream was to become a pilot . Due to his nearsightedness however, he almost went on to abandon his dream but because of Caproni, an Italian airplane designer, his inspiring words instead drove Jiro to become an airplane designer.

Many years have passed since then, as Jiro dedicated studying on airplane design, he eventually made it to university when an earthquake was happening at the same time. This incident led to Jiro meeting Naoko for the first time, the starting point of their short-lasting yet deep relationship. Soon after the earthquake incident, Jiro studied abroad along with his friend Kiro. It was during this time that Jiro had started his attempts in executing his airplane designs. After a few failed attempts, Jiro went on vacation and found Naoko again after a couple of months have passed. Jiro and Naoko went on to further strengthen their relationship as it was in the end. Unfortunately, Naoko died of tuberculosis the day of Jiro's test flight. Jiro Horikoshi's story is one that embodies dreams and aspirations, despite the numerous tragedies that came along with it.
